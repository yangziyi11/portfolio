EEDP in GEA

The needs for it is to design a logo for Edison Engineering Development Program in GE Appliances. They want me to put emphasize on the enginnering element. Thus, I did several iterations on elements including screw (engineering), bulb (invention), and Edison (origin).

A Bench for Caps Program

Led by AAF, GEA is partnering with Newburg Middle School on a plastics recycling project. Students will collect plastic bottle caps that will be recycled into a park bench. The bench will be produced by Green Tree Plastics, a commercial plastics manufacturer located in southern Indiana. For this project we also want to engage all GEA Louisville employees to contribute their plastic caps to this effort. The poster should serve this purpose.
