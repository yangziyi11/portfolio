Push-to-talk App design

This is a B2B project between Samsung and AT&T to design a Push-to-talk (PTT) application for first responders.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

We have a team with around 3 designers and several contractors. Each designer handle his or her feature set from requirement review, concept generation, low fidelity wirefram to GUI design delievery. We also work with ATT content team for contents and ATT branding team for icons.

As for the project iteself, when we think about first responders, we think of some bulky walkie talkies. They are reliable, and first responders are very familiar with them. However, with the rise of the new generation, people use smart phones every day. It could be easy for first responders to take the change and switch to Push-to-talk applicaitons on a smart phone. Also, smart phones have so many potentials than traditional LMR devices, because it can carry more features. For example, text message, file transferring, video chat, location sharing and etc.

It might be a trend for Push-to-talk App with accessories to replace traditional Push-to-talk devices.
