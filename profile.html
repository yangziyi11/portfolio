01. SKETCHING AND IDEATION

For the ideation phase, I always start from pen and paper. It could help me to organize my thoughts and get more ideas to start with.

Other pen compatible digital devices also helped me a lot. They are more portable than pen and paper.

02. DESIGN TOOLS

I use Sketch or Figma, and Photoshop to flesh my concepts. For prototyping, I prefer InVision, Framer.js and Hype.

For 3D modeling, and scene building, I use Maya and Unity.

03. DATA ANALYSIS

Coming from human factor engineering background to HCI, I am proficient in data analysis, questionnaire design, experiment design, and conducting user testing.

04. MY DESIGN PROCESS

Design is inquiry and action. It is both product and process.

Design is an iterative process. I like to design based on evidence, making lean and effective design. However, design is not engineering, there are times we have to make assumptions and guesses. Empathy is the key.
