3D Modeling Project

Tools: Unity, Maya, CAVE

CONCEPT SKETCHES

PROJECT STATEMENT

As I stated in my artist statement and concept book, I want to create a world that reflects on the real relationship among people and between human and nature.

Pay for the Meal

I presented my work with the help of CAVE. Audience could wear VR glasse and use game controller to navigate through my scene.

To share the work with you, I exported the game with webGL. You could click the icon below to access the game. If you are using mobile devices, you could click to view the screen-recorded video.
